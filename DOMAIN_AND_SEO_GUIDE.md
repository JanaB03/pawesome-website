## 🌐 Optional: Custom Domain Setup

**If you want your own custom domain** (like `pawesomeadventurewalks.co.uk` instead of the Vercel subdomain):

### Step 1: Register a Domain
**Recommended domain names:**
- `pawesomeadventurewalks.co.uk` (UK-specific, best for local SEO)
- `pawesomeadventurewalks.com` (International)
- `pawesomewalks.co.uk` (Shorter option)

**Best UK Domain Registrars:**
1. **123-reg.co.uk** - Popular in UK, good support
2. **GoDaddy.co.uk** - Well-known, easy to use
3. **Namecheap.com** - Affordable, good value

**Cost:** Usually £10-15/year for .co.uk domains

### Step 2: Connect Domain to Vercel
1. Log into your Vercel account
2. Go to your project settings
3. Click "Domains"
4. Add your custom domain
5. Follow Vercel's instructions to update DNS records
6. Wait 24-48 hours for DNS to propagate

**Note:** Your site will work on both URLs (vercel.app and your custom domain)

## 🔍 Making Your Site Searchable on Google

### ✅ Step 1: URLs Already Updated!
I've already updated all your files with your Vercel domain: `https://pawesomekelso.vercel.app`

### Step 2: Create Google Search Console Account

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Add Property"
4. Enter your website URL (e.g., `https://pawesomeadventurewalks.co.uk`)
5. Verify ownership using one of these methods:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - **HTML tag** (add to your index.html)
   - **DNS verification** (if you have access)

### Step 3: Submit Your Sitemap

1. In Google Search Console, go to "Sitemaps"
2. Enter: `https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
4. Wait 24-48 hours for Google to crawl your site

### Step 4: Request Indexing

1. In Google Search Console, use "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter your homepage URL
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Repeat for important pages (services, contact, etc.)

### Step 5: Google Business Profile (CRITICAL for Local SEO)

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/)
2. Create or claim your business listing
3. Add:
   - Business name: "Pawesome Adventure Walks"
   - Category: "Pet Sitter" or "Dog Walker"
   - Address: Your service area (Kelso, surrounding areas)
   - Phone: 07583 629261
   - Website: Your new domain
   - Hours: Your operating hours
   - Photos: Add your logo and pet photos
4. Verify your business (usually by postcard or phone)

**This is ESSENTIAL for local search!**
