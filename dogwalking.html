Professional Dog Walking

Keep your dog healthy, happy, and well-exercised with our flexible walking service. Walks can be tailored to suit your schedule and your dog's needs, ensuring they get the outdoor time they love.

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Strictly professional lead handling
  • Regular photo and message updates
  • Meet and greet before walks begin
  • Portable pet shower for muddy paws
  • Fresh water provided when needed

Pricing

£15 per walk*

+ £10 for each additional dog from the same household

What Makes Our Dog Walking Service Special

Meet and Greet

We always start with a friendly meet and greet to make sure your dog feels comfortable and to give you peace of mind before their walks begin. This initial visit allows us to understand your dog's personality, preferences, and any special requirements they may have.

Muddy Paws? No Problem!

No need to worry about muddy paws — we've got it covered! We carry a portable pet shower, perfect for rinsing off after a fun walk, and have towels on hand for drying too. Your home stays clean, and your dog stays happy.

Hydration & Care

Fresh water is always provided when needed, especially on warm days, to keep your dog happy, healthy, and hydrated throughout their walk. We pay close attention to your pet's needs and ensure they're comfortable in all weather conditions.

* Prices shown are standard rates. Additional charges may apply for services on weekends, bank holidays, and public holidays. Please contact us for specific pricing.
